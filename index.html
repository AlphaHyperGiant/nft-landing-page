Browse APKs on iPhone. Install on Android.

Search, filter, and open app pages. On iOS you can share or copy an APK download link to send it to an Android device.

Important: iPhones and iPads can’t install APK files. This marketplace is for discovery and sharing Android downloads.

How it works on iPhone

  1. Browse apps here and open an app to view details.
  2. Tap Share (if available) or Copy link.
  3. Send the link to your Android phone (Messages, WhatsApp, email, etc.).
  4. Download and install the APK on Android (with appropriate security checks).
